About Us

We are Swifgoo Corporation a company specialized in Project Supply, Procurement Management and Industrial Supply for large Engineering, Powering, and Automotive and Manufacturing Industries.

  • Our customers get optimum and long lasting performance from their machines.
  • We Provide all types of Flexible Couplings and his Genuine Spares.
  • Focus on customer requirements, quality standards, competitive pricing and delivering well within time lines.

Swifgoo Corporation is having a team of well qualified and experienced design engineers. Along with design team, there are also other teams like Production, maintenance, Inspection, sales and after sales-services teams

We have a large setup of Fabrication & In house Powder Coating Plant. Our Powder Coating Plant is fully equipped with conveyor & 7 tank cleaning & surface preparation process. We have Ovens which can accommodate large parts with dimension 8 feet x 6 feet x 22 feet.
